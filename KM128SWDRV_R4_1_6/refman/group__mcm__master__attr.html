MCM Crossbar Master Access Attributes
MCM Driver

Overview

List of crossbar master access attributes a privileged/user and secure/nonsecure master attribute configurations.

Note
Loading MCM_MASTER_EN... attributes forces the master to simply use the internally-generated two attributes. Internal attributes are controlled by UserMode and SpvrMode macros.

Macros

#define MCM_MASTER_FR_PRIV_SECURE
 Master forced to {privileged, secure} (default)
 
#define MCM_MASTER_FR_USER_SECURE
 Master forced to {user, secure}.
 
#define MCM_MASTER_FR_USER_NONSEC
 Master forced to {user, nonsecure}.
 
#define MCM_MASTER_EN_PRIV_OR_USER_FR_SECURE
 Enable {privileged or user} and force {secure}.
 
#define MCM_MASTER_EN_PRIV_OR_USER_FR_NONSEC
 Enable {privileged or user} and force {nonsecure}.
 
#define MCM_MASTER_EN_PRIV_OR_USER_SECURE_OR_NONSEC
 Enable {privileged or user, secure or nonsecure}.
