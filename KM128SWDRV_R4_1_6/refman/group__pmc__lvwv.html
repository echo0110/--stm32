PMC Low-Voltage Warning Voltages

Overview

List of low-voltage warning voltages.

Macros

#define PMC_LVW1
 Low trip point is 2.7 V if PMC_LVDH (low-voltage detect high range) selected otherwise it is 1.8 V.
 
#define PMC_LVW2
 Mid1 trip point is 2.8 V if PMC_LVDH (low-voltage detect high range) selected otherwise it is 1.9 V.
 
#define PMC_LVW3
 Mid2 trip point is 2.9 V if PMC_LVDH (low-voltage detect high range) selected otherwise it is 2.0 V.
 
#define PMC_LVW4
 High trip point is 3.0 V if PMC_LVDH (low-voltage detect high range) selected otherwise it is 2.1 V.
