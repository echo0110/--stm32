XBAR Example
XBAR Driver

This example shows typical use of the Inter-Peripheral Crossbar Switch (XBAR) module.

Source code:
/******************************************************************************
* (c) Copyright 2010-2015, Freescale Semiconductor Inc.
* ALL RIGHTS RESERVED.
******************************************************************************
* xbar_test.c
******************************************************************************/
#include "drivers.h"
void main(void)
{
SIM_EnableModule (XBAR);
XBAR_Init (XBAR_MODULE_NO_EDGE_DETECT_CONFIG, PRI_LVL0, NULL);
XBAR_Path (XBAR_LOGIC0, XBAR_DMA);
while(1);
}
